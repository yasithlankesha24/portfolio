  • Save Hearts - 2022
    The National Blood Transfusion Service in Sri Lanka is a government-run organization that supplies blood and blood products to both government and private hospitals across the country. The service is made up of 24 cluster centers, 106 hospital-based blood banks, and 2 independent blood centers. However, 102 of the hospital-based blood banks are currently operational. The organization is facing a problem where they are struggling to effectively organize blood donation camps, anticipate the amount of blood needed in the future, or notify donors of urgent blood requirements. This makes it difficult for them to effectively use their resources and meet the demand for blood. The proposed web application aims to give solutions for these problems by providing a system to forecast blood amount, a notification system for urgent blood needs, and a system to manage blood donation camps.
